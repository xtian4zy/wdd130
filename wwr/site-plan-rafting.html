Overview

Purpose

The rafting website main purpose is to share opinions to the public about our rafting packages and share participation opportunities with them.

Audience

The primary audience of the website include: individuals who are seeking to know about rafting and how to participate in the it. They cut across all demography (old, young, rich, poor, working or retired individuals). These people could be familiar with rafting or are first timers who want to explore rafting. They will be seeking information about rafting packages that are available and the various times of the year when they can participate, including the cost and available safety measures.
